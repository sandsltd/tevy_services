When it comes to alloy wheel refurbishment in Exeter, choosing between mobile and workshop-based services is a crucial decision. Each option offers distinct advantages and suits different situations. Let's explore which solution best matches your needs.

## Mobile Service

### Perfect For

- Minor to moderate damage
- Single wheel repairs
- Convenient locations
- Time-sensitive repairs

### Limitations

- Weather dependent
- Limited repair types
- Basic equipment only
- Space requirements

## Workshop Service

### Perfect For

- Complete restorations
- Diamond cut finish
- Multiple wheels
- Complex repairs

### Considerations

- Requires transport
- Longer turnaround
- Booking required
- Higher cost

## Exeter Price Comparison

### Mobile Services

- Minor scuff repairs: £30-£50 per wheel
- Full wheel repaint: £60-£80 per wheel
- Kerb damage repair: £40-£60 per wheel

### Workshop Services

- Standard refurbishment: £70-£90 per wheel
- Diamond cut finish: £90-£120 per wheel
- Custom finishes: £80-£150 per wheel

## Making Your Choice

### Choose Mobile If:

- You need [convenient mobile refurbishment](/mobile-alloy-wheel-refurbishment)
- The damage is minor
- You have a suitable location
- Time is a priority

### Choose Workshop If:

- You want [diamond cut finish](/blog/diamond-cut-vs-painted-alloys)
- Multiple wheels need work
- Significant damage exists
- Premium result is priority
